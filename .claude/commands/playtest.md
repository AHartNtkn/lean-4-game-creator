# /playtest

Use when you want a serious quality audit.

## Prompt text

Audit this lean4game content as if the goal were to ship something that genuinely teaches well.

Check:
- technical sanity,
- coverage closure,
- granularity,
- inventory design,
- hint quality,
- proof-move teaching,
- transfer,
- and role fit (lecture, pset, boss, etc.).

Simulate at least:
- an attentive novice,
- and a somewhat experienced Lean user.

Be severe about “technically correct but educationally weak”.

Return exactly these sections:

1. Overall verdict
2. Rubric scores
3. Coverage gaps
4. Granularity defects
5. Learner simulation notes
6. Boss or pset integrity notes as relevant
7. Technical risks
8. Ranked patch list
9. What to playtest again after patching
