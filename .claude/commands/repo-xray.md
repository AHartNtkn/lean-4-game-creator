# /repo-xray

Use when you have an existing lean4game repo and want to extend, repair, or imitate it without style drift.

## Prompt text

Read this lean4game repo as a curriculum artifact, not just a codebase.

Your tasks:

1. infer the repo’s house style,
2. infer how it chooses coverage,
3. infer how it chooses granularity,
4. identify its world archetypes,
5. identify its inventory policy,
6. identify its boss formula,
7. identify likely learner pain points,
8. and identify what future contributors must preserve.

Do not give me generic advice. Use the repo itself as evidence.

Return exactly these sections:

1. House style summary
2. Coverage logic
3. Granularity logic
4. World archetypes
5. Level archetypes
6. Inventory and doc policy
7. Hint policy
8. Boss formula
9. Style fractures and course-design risks
10. Contributor constraints
11. Best insertion points for new material
